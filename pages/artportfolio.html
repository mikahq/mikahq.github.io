Design journey

I am well-versed in traditional art, like painting.

(Acrylic, gouache, watercolor, oils, oil pastels, crayons, colored pencils...

Around high school I was given a Huion tablet, which taught me the commands for creating digital art. I've since traded it in for Procreate and Glyphs to make digital art and learn about typography.

I do some short animations, hand-drawn. I've picked up some marketing skills and use my design background for those kinds of projects. But I think my heart is really in design!
