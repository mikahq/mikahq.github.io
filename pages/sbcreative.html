Productivity App

Designing a productivity and habit tracking platform for competitive yet tired college students.

Skills

Product design, Research, User interviews

Tools

Figma, Google Forms

Timeline: 3 months (October 2023 - December 2023)

The Problem

Habit and goal tracking apps are popular, but often lack features which incentivize users to stay on the app. While features desirable for habit tracking apps, such as Pomodoro timers or calendar views are integrated into other apps, we have yet to see one which combines multiple features for its user.

Groundwork

In order to form a new habit or maintain an older one, internal motivation is crucial. One important motivator is social accountability, which allows for a second view in celebrating new or old habits. Therefore, how can people trying to form a goal start & maintain their habits through the aid of technology and the presence of social accountability?

Research

Our team sent out a survey to UCSB students to assess the attitude towards habit trackers. We found five key points defined their experiences:

  • Separating tasks & events

  • Interviewees showed tendency to use different trackers for calendar events and tasks to do

  • People indicated they used color tagging and progress bars frequently

  • Most people said they used similar apps for short term goals, also informative for designing tasks and events!

  • 73.5% of our respondents noted they would benefit from “having a single location where you track your habits”

Key Changes

All features accessible through home screen (task/event creation, timer, group placements, tasks/events of the day)

Carousel of groups + leaderboard for each

Users have the option to click between different weeks to see future tasks/events (highlight bar will indicate current week selection)

Calendar will show colored dot labels based on types of events there are that day

All completed tasks for that week will be automatically moved to the bottom with an option to deselect if uncompleted/needs to be redone

Ability to access and edit tasks directly from task page by clicking on specified task/event
