I had the pleasure to act as an experimenter and research assistant for the SCD lab, a wonderful lab which explores how children navigate their social world. I created a poster to communicate scientifc findings, conducted independent data analyes and visualizations, & helped with logo design.

The SCD Lab contributes to research investigating the building blocks of social cognition.

I joined the SCD lab in 2022 as my introduction to experimental psychology. As I began to code videos, help with data collection, and conduct literature reviews, I also became interested in learning how to code data in the social sciences using R, and also how to ask questions of my own.
