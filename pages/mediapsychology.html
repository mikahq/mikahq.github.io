Research: Spatial Communication in Team Environments

I got to conduct research on how players in virtual environments leverage technology and language to communicate about their environment!

Skills

User research, Data Visualization and Analyses, Science Communication

Tools

R, Twitch, Excel

In 2020, the electronic sports (esports) market accrued 1.1 billion USD in sales around the world, rising in popularity (Newzoo, 2019). Many multiplayer games revolve around objectives based on teamwork; in teams, people engage in prosocial behavior, collaborating with others to achieve a common goal (Hu & Liden, 2015).

When we cooperate we assume a communicative responsibility, incorporating and accounting for other people's efforts to create a shared understanding of the situation (Aune et al., 2005; Youk & Park, 2023).

Question:

What leads to efficient spatial communication in teams within a goal-orientated virtual world, both linguistically and as afforded by technology?
