Data Visualizations

Welcome to my data gallery! All graphs are done in R.

This model was created to observe the "life-dinner principle": in co-evolutionary arms races, prey animals have the advantage in that they are fighting for their lives whereas predators are just fighting for dinner.

This model was made to investigate whether assortative mating can emerge from individual mating motivations. Agents will select one another as mates based on a physical attractiveness variable. This model is based loosely on Kalick and Hamilton (1986).

A model of infectious disease transmission. Agents will move around a 2-dimensional surface and potentially spread an infectious disease to those nearby. The model will manipulate the number of agents who move vs. stay in place (i.e. social distance) and observe the effect of social distancing on disease spread.

This is an agent based model. The purpose of this model is to explore the emergence of fads as a function of varying levels of conformity and anticonformity using facial hair trends over time.

This is an agent based model of the Schelling segregation model. As long as individuals do not want to be the minority in their local neighborhood, the agents will have the desire to be in different local environments.
